Why Cal Poly SLO is a Great School

Cal Poly San Luis Obispo is known for its Learn by Doing philosophy, outstanding academic programs, and vibrant campus life. Students benefit from hands-on experiences, supportive faculty, and beautiful surroundings.

"The school does a great job of getting employers on campus and interacting with students through a variety of career fairs and clubs."
- Chris Schrader Cal Poly Alumni

Here are some reasons to consider Cal Poly SLO:
1. Excellent Engineering and Architecture programs
2. Beautiful campus near the California coast
3. Strong community and student support
